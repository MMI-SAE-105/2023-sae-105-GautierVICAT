Seagram Building

The Seagram Building, located in New York, is an iconic skyscraper designed by the German architect Mies van der Rohe in collaboration with the American architect Philip Johnson. Erected between 1954 and 1958, it stands as a masterpiece of architectural modernism. This glass and steel building is characterized by its minimalist fa√ßade and innovative use of materials.
