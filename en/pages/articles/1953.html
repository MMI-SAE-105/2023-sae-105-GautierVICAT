MoMA Sculpture Garden

The MoMA Sculpture Garden is an outdoor space at the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) in New York, showcasing a collection of modern and contemporary sculptures within a landscaped setting. It provides an outdoor artistic experience in the heart of Manhattan, fostering a dynamic interaction between art, space, and visitors. This sculpture garden is distinct from the work of the renowned architect Philip Johnson, but it also reflects MoMA's commitment to modern art, a movement closely connected to Johnson as both an architect and a collector.
