Glass Canvas Exhibition

Image of the Fondation Cartier

The exhibition will take place at Fondation Cartier, 261Bd Raspail, 75014 Paris. It will run from April 13 to June 23, with the following schedule: open daily from 11 am to 8 pm, except on Mondays. On Tuesdays, there will be evening hours until 10 pm. The galleries start closing 15 minutes before the end of the day, at 9:45 pm on Tuesdays, and at 7:45 pm on other days.

Here are the different rates :

  • Full rate at €11
  • Reduced rate at €7.50
  • Youth rates (13-25 years old) at €5
  • Fondation Cartier Pass at €18-50
  • For individuals under 13, holding a pass, LCOM card, press card, disability card, Fondation Cartier Pass, and for students, entry is free.
