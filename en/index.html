Philip C. Johnson, the glass architect

Welcome to the website prepared for an exhibition dedicated to the visionary architect, Philip Johnson. Explore the iconic creations of this 20th-century pioneer, such as the Glass House and the Lipstick Building, which have redefined architecture. Dive into his varied influences, from modernist movements to postmodern experiments. Celebrating Johnson's timeless ingenuity, this site offers a captivating immersion into his lasting impact on global architecture. Join us in anticipation of the exhibition paying tribute to this architect who pushed the boundaries of creativity.
